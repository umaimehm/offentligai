Who are we ?

Offentlig A.I is an alliance of Norwegian offentlig (state) companies who are either working or planning to work with Artifical Intelligence projects.

In our experience, most companies face more or less the same issues while working with A.I projects. The issues can range from simple problems like "how to get started with A.I projects" or they could be as complex as "how to fine tune a machine learning algorithm".

We believe we can help each other through collaboration - knowledge sharing and expert help.

This is the biggest alliance of AI professionals in Norway. Currently we are a group of 150+ members who represent 35 state companies. Our memebers have a varied skillset and work with varuius roles such as: developers, archietcts, team leads, managers etc..
