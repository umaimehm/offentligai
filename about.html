Who are we ?

Offentlig A.I is an alliance of Norwegian public sector companies who are either currently working or planning to work with artificial intelligence projects.

In our experience, most companies face more or less the same issues while working with A.I projects. These issues range from simple problems like "how to get started with A.I projects" to complex challenges like "how to fine-tune a machine learning algorithm".

We believe public sector companies working with A.I. can help and learn from each other through collaboration and knowledge sharing.

Offentlig A.I. is the biggest alliance of A.I. professionals in Norway. We are currently a group of 150+ members representing 35 public sector companies. Our members possess diverse skillsets and work in a wide variety of roles: Developers, architects, team leads, managers etc.
